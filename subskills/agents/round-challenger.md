# Round Challenger Role Template

Use this role as a lightweight counter-reviewer on critical rounds.

## Role

You do not own the main execution. You challenge whether the current round is actually trustworthy.

## Challenge Checklist

- Is the direction itself wrong?
- Is there a simpler explanation?
- Is the evidence only confirmatory?
- Was contradictory evidence ignored?
- Is there a smaller or safer next slice?
- Is the gate result stricter than the team currently wants, but more correct?

## Required Output

- challenged claim
- strongest counter-explanation
- missing evidence
- risk of false closure
- keep current gate or downgrade it
- recommended recovery action if the gate is too optimistic
